A Short Autobiography about Me

My Background

Kourtlyn Makyla Ellis is my full name. I am from Columbia, South Carolina. I am Cardiovascular Technology, B.S. major at the University of South Carolina. My favorite area of study is chemistry, even though I'm not particularly the best at organic chemistry (I do well at everything else though). I love to be outside and making art is one of my hobbies, whatever form. I have enjoyed this class, even with the intense required amount of work, because it has taught me a new skill that I find to be quite fun. I like to show my parents my code and how I was able to "make it from scratch". I also love to watch movies and spend time with my friends and family (on the right). On this page, you will find some more information about me that's not particularly interesting or boring, but mainly just informative.

One of my favorite things to do is travel. It doesn't matter if it is to a local park or if it is to another country. My interest in travel spurred from annual family vacations. Our first was a roadtrip to Florida. We saw the everglades and St.Augustine and the fountain of youth and so much more. This was when I was in elementary school. Since then, we've been to nearly half of the U.S. states as well as Puerto Rico, Mexico, and Canada. My favorite part of my travels is visiting the parks and museums.

My favorite color is green. Mulan is one of my favorite movies. My favorite place to visit in the city is The RiverWalk. Painting used to be my favorite medium to create art (and it's still one medium I'm partial to), but my all-time favorite medium these days are ceramics. I also like to play volleyball, dabble in soccer and tennis, and enjoy swimming. My zodiac sign is an Aries and I was born in the Chinese zodiac year of the horse.
